# Presentation Design -- Tony's Reference

## Slide Design Principles
- **One idea per slide.** If it needs two ideas, make two slides.
- **Visual hierarchy**: Title (what it is) > Key visual (show it) > Supporting text (explain it)
- **Data slides**: One chart + one sentence takeaway. Never a wall of numbers.
- **Text slides**: Max 5 bullet points, max 7 words each. Slides support speech, not replace it.

## Harman's Presentation Style
- Clean, modern aesthetic. No clip art. No busy backgrounds.
- School branding where appropriate (colors, logo)
- Heavy use of images (students, school life, real moments)
- Builds/reveals for complex information (don't show everything at once)
- Section dividers with the framework letter/word

## Slide Structures by Purpose
- **Opening**: Full-bleed image + title overlay. Set the mood.
- **Framework intro**: The acronym/word in large type. Clean.
- **Content section**: Title (the letter) + story + image OR title + data + takeaway
- **Quote slide**: Large quote text + attribution. Minimal decoration.
- **Closing**: Circle back to opening image. Final message.
